My Furry Friend

Sofie the Shih Tzu

A Few Things About Her...

Sofie is small, but she is mighty.
She likes to sleep all day, which keeps everything tidy.
There are days where she doesn't move off of the couch,
And we don't bug her because she will then be a grouch.

She will eat anything in sight, especially if it's chicken.
Give her a few pieces and you will get so many kisses!
She also enjoys ice cream, and if she were a human, it would make her scream!

When it's actually time for bed, she will curl right up beside you.
It doesn't take much for her to fall asleep- I guarantee you she won't make a peep.
Sofie is the best furry friend, one that I can always turn to.
She is small, but she will love you!

The End!
